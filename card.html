The Magestic Seal

About

Seal milk is over 50% fat so the new born pups can gain 30kg in their first two weeks of life.

  • Common Name: Grey Seal
  • Latin Name: Halichoerus grypus
  • Lifespan: Males 25yr Females 35yr
  • Gestation period: 9 Months + 3 Months delayed implatation

The grey seal is the largest breeding mammal found in the UK and 40% of the world's population is found in the waters surrounding the UK.
